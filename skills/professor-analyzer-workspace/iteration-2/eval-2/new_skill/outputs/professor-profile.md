## Research focus

Fei-Fei Li is a foundational figure in computer vision (creator of ImageNet). Her current
research center of gravity is **spatial intelligence** — AI that perceives depth, understands
3D scenes, navigates, and interacts with the physical world — alongside **embodied AI / robot
learning** and **ambient intelligence for healthcare delivery**. She co-directs Stanford HAI
(Human-Centered AI Institute) and is co-founder/CEO of World Labs, a startup explicitly built
around spatial intelligence and 3D/generative world models.

Her stated interests (Stanford profile): AI, machine learning, deep learning, computer vision,
robotic learning, and ambient intelligence for healthcare. The lab frames its agenda around
visual *understanding, reasoning, and generation*.

**Important for this applicant:** none of her documented research is in natural language
processing, multilingual large language models, or low-resource languages. Her LLM-adjacent
work is on *vision-language* and *embodied/world* models, not text-centric multilingual NLP.
