## Description

The EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Statistics and Machine Learning (StatML)
is a fully-funded, cohort-based doctoral programme jointly run by Imperial College
London and the University of Oxford. It is a four-year integrated PhD (Imperial) /
DPhil (Oxford) training researchers in statistics and statistical machine learning,
with methodological and theoretical work as well as applications. The 2026-27 cycle
is closed; **admissions for 2027(-28) entry open later in 2026.** Projects are
shaped with supervisors across the two departments, several of whom work on graph-
and geometry-based ML methods.

## Requirements

- Strong degree in statistics, mathematics, computer science or a closely related
  quantitative field.
- Apply via the Oxford and/or Imperial admissions portal, selecting the StatML CDT
  course (Oxford course code G1703.1, "EPSRC CDT in Statistics and Machine Learning
  (PhD 4YFT)").
- Verify English-language and specific entry requirements on the StatML site.

## Why it fits

Matches the applicant's filters: UK location, **2027 entry cycle**, and **fully

span statistical ML including geometric/graph methods, so a graph neural network or
geometric deep learning thesis can be pursued with the right supervisor. A strong,
prestigious funded option alongside the Edinburgh CDT.
