## Important framing note (please read)

I verified every item below against live pages. One honest finding up front: **as of June 2026, almost all individually-advertised GNN/GDL studentships are recruiting for 2026 starts, not 2027.** The PhD calendar is the reason — most named projects are advertised 3–9 months before they start, so genuine "starting 2027" adverts will mostly appear from **autumn 2026 onward.**

For a 2027 start with guaranteed full funding, the realistic route right now is the **structured doctoral programmes** (UK UKRI CDTs and the pan-European ELLIS network), whose 2027-entry application windows open in autumn 2026. I've listed both: (A) live fully-funded openings you can act on now (mostly 2026, some flexible into 2027), and (B) 2027-entry programmes to track / register for now.

Funding terminology check: in the UK, "fully funded" = full tuition + UKRI-rate tax-free stipend (£20,780 in 2025/26; some TechExpert-pilot CDTs pay £31,000). Many FindAPhD GNN hits are labelled **"Self-Funded Students Only"** — those are explicitly NOT funded and are excluded from the funded list below.
